SUMMARY
Kevin Samla is an Illustrator that uses both digital and traditional mediums. He is experienced in using watercolors,ink, and pencils as well as programs like Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, and Adobe Indesign. He has experience illustrating komik books, book covers, posters and other promotionals.

He is also a writer focusing on writing short stories, and spoken word poetry. As a result not only he can work well with text he is also very articulate in both Filipino and English.

He is currently honing is his skills creating concept art as well as writing stories and komikbooks
